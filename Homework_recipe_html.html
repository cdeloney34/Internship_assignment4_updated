How to make a Good Steak

Ingredients:

    2 Ribeye steaks 1″ thick
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable
  • Steak seasoning or kosher salt & pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter or herbed butter
  • ribeye steaks 1″ thick

Instructions

  • 1.Remove steaks from the fridge at least 45 minutes before cooking.
  • 2.Just before cooking, rub steaks with olive oil and generously season to taste.
  • 3.Preheat grill to medium heat (approximately 375°F).
  • 4.Prepare steaks as above and grill 5-6 minutes per side for medium-rare or 6-7 minutes per side for medium.
  • 5.Remove from the grill and top with a pat of butter, loosely tent with foil. Rest steaks 5-10 minutes before serving.
  • 6.Heat a large cast-iron pan on the stove over medium-high heat.
  • 7.Add steaks and cook 2 minutes per side to brown. Place the pan in the oven and roast 11-14 minutes or until the ribeyes reache the desired temperature.
  • 8.Immediately remove steaks from the pan and place on a plate to rest. Top steaks with a pat of butter, loosely tent with foil.
  • 9.Rest steaks 5-10 minutes before serving.
